LEED Tips

LEED Certification stands for Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design and is a prestigious honor bestowed upon green buildings that are energy efficient and have a low environmental impact. Achieving LEED Certification is both a way to preserve our planet's resources while also receiving federal tax breaks in return. Browse our site to learn about the various ways to achieve points toward LEED Certification on both new and existing buildings, both commercial and residential.
